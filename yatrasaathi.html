About Us

YatraSaathi is a big step towards women empowerment. YatraSaathi provides tourists with local women (SAATHI) that guides them to travel and helps them to shop securely without any frauds by the shopkeepers. At each destination set by tourist we provide a saathi.

Any woman can register herself as a saathi and earn rewards.

OUR SERVICES

SHOPPING GUIDES

Local women register themselves as guides and when a tourist sends a request we appoint a saathi to assist them with shopping.
